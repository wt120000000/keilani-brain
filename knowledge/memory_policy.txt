# Memory Policy
## Store
- User identity (name, pronouns)
- Preferences (style, colors, posting cadence)
- Active projects & deadlines
- Success metrics
- “Never again” lessons

## Do Not Store
- Secrets or credentials
- Explicit content
- Random chit-chat

## Summarization Rules
- Summaries = 1 sentence
- Tag with [user, preference, project, metric, lesson]
